⚡ Set Up Automatic Weekly Payments on Venmo

Save yourself from remembering every Sunday by setting up a recurring payment:

  1. Open the Venmo app on your phone
  2. Tap Pay or Request at the bottom
  3. Search for @hduff13
  4. Enter your weekly amount ($30 or $50)
  5. Tap the calendar icon next to the amount
  6. Select Weekly and choose Sunday as the day
  7. Add a note like "Weekly class payment" and tap Pay

You can cancel or adjust recurring payments anytime in your Venmo settings under Scheduled Payments.

Client Agreement & Policies

Please read each policy carefully before signing. This agreement covers payments, scheduling, cancellations, and expectations.

Session Packages & Payment

Payment is required before participation in any training sessions. Session packages must be used within the expiration timeframe. For virtual sessions, Zoom access links will only be sent once payment has been received and confirmed.

Scheduling

Clients will schedule sessions based on available training times. For virtual sessions, clients are responsible for logging into scheduled sessions on time. Late arrivals may result in shortened sessions in order to maintain the schedule for other clients.

Missed Sessions & Cancellations

Clients should provide advance notice if they cannot attend a scheduled session. Missed sessions without notice may result in forfeiture of that session.

Missed Virtual Sessions

If a client is unable to attend a scheduled virtual training session, a recording of the session will be provided. Recorded session links will remain active for 7 days from the date of the class. Recorded sessions are provided for active clients only and are not intended to be shared or distributed.

Zoom Access & Session Participation

Zoom links are provided to registered Everyday Strong clients only. Session links may not be shared with others. Clients who share access links or allow non-registered participants to join sessions may lose access to future sessions.

Virtual Training Requirements

Clients are responsible for ensuring they have a safe workout space, appropriate equipment if needed, and a reliable internet connection.

Client Communication

Clients are encouraged to communicate openly about injuries, discomfort or pain, medical conditions, and scheduling conflicts. Everyday Strong will make reasonable efforts to respond to messages within 24 to 48 hours during normal business days.

Results Disclaimer

Results vary between individuals based on many factors including effort, consistency, nutrition, sleep, and lifestyle habits. Specific results cannot be guaranteed.

Respectful Training Environment

Everyday Strong is committed to providing a supportive and respectful environment. Clients are expected to treat trainers and fellow participants with respect during all sessions.
