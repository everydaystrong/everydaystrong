⚡ Set Up Automatic Weekly Payments on Venmo

Save yourself from remembering every Sunday by setting up a recurring payment:

  1. Open the Venmo app on your phone
  2. Tap Pay or Request at the bottom
  3. Search for @hduff13
  4. Enter your weekly amount ($30 or $50)
  5. Tap the calendar icon next to the amount
  6. Select Weekly and choose Sunday as the day
  7. Add a note like "Weekly class payment" and tap Pay

You can cancel or adjust recurring payments anytime in your Venmo settings under Scheduled Payments.

Client Agreement & Policies

Please read each policy carefully before signing. This agreement covers payments, scheduling, cancellations, and expectations.

Class Access & Payment

Payment is required before participation in any classes. Membership must be kept current to continue attending live classes and accessing the member dashboard. For virtual classes, Zoom access will only be sent once payment has been received and confirmed.

Scheduling

Clients will attend classes based on the published class schedule. For virtual classes, clients are responsible for logging in on time. Late arrivals may result in shortened participation in order to maintain the schedule for other clients.

Missed Classes & Cancellations

Clients should provide advance notice if they cannot attend a scheduled class. A recording of the class will be posted to the client's member dashboard, so missed live attendance does not mean missing the content.

Class Recordings

Recordings of live classes are posted to each client's member dashboard and remain available there for as long as the client maintains an active membership. Recordings are provided for active clients only and are not intended to be shared, downloaded, or distributed outside the platform.

Zoom Access & Dashboard Use

Live class Zoom links and dashboard access are provided to registered Everyday Strong clients only and may not be shared with others. Clients who share Zoom links, recordings, or dashboard access with non-registered individuals may lose access to future classes.

Virtual Training Requirements

Clients are responsible for ensuring they have a safe workout space, appropriate equipment if needed, and a reliable internet connection.

Client Communication

Clients are encouraged to communicate openly about injuries, discomfort or pain, medical conditions, and scheduling conflicts. Everyday Strong will make reasonable efforts to respond to messages within 24 to 48 hours during normal business days.

Results Disclaimer

Results vary between individuals based on many factors including effort, consistency, nutrition, sleep, and lifestyle habits. Specific results cannot be guaranteed.

Respectful Training Environment

Everyday Strong is committed to providing a supportive and respectful environment. Clients are expected to treat trainers and fellow participants with respect during all classes.
