About Everyday Strong

Strength isn't a phase.
It's a lifestyle.

At Everyday Strong, we believe in building real, lasting strength through structured, sustainable training that fits real life. We move away from extremes and focus instead on consistency, progression, and accountability.

Everyday Strong was created to offer a balanced approach to strength training one that supports long term results rather than short term intensity.

I lead virtual coaching, providing structured semi private sessions and individualized programming designed to support consistency and confidence from anywhere.

Because true strength should carry into everyday life not just the gym.

Meet Your Trainer

Hi, I'm Heather!

I'm a mom of two boys (14 and 8), and like many of the people I work with, I've had to learn how to balance health, family, work, and everything in between.

Over the past six years, I've lost 80 pounds, and that journey has taught me that progress isn't perfect. It's full of ups, downs, restarts, and learning along the way. I'm human too, and I believe fitness should be a safe, supportive place where people feel understood, not judged.

My goal with Everyday Strong is to create a space where real people can show up as they are, build strength, and work toward becoming stronger in a way that fits their everyday life.
